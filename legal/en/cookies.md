# Cookie Policy

## 1. What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file placed on your device (computer, phone, tablet) when you visit a website. It allows the site to remember certain information about your browsing to improve your experience or to comply with legal obligations.

## 2. Cookies used by this site

This site uses only **essential technical cookies**. No advertising, profiling or behavioural analytics cookies are placed without your prior consent.

| Name | Type | Duration | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| `cookie-consent` | Essential | 12 months | Stores your cookie choice (accepted / declined) |

## 3. Third-party services

This site integrates the following external services, which may place their own cookies or make requests to their servers:

- **Stripe** — during the payment process only. Stripe may place cookies necessary for security and fraud prevention. These cookies are governed by [Stripe's privacy policy](https://stripe.com/en-be/privacy).

You can also configure your browser to refuse or delete all cookies. Here is how to access the relevant settings:

- **Chrome**: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox**: Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari**: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
- **Edge**: Settings → Privacy, search and services → Cookies

Refusing certain cookies may affect the proper functioning of some site features (in particular the booking and payment process).
